A Vogue Idea: The Starlet Issue

Dora Chow on music, fashion, and her new life in LA

Dora Chow is a fashion doll I made right after the prototype for "Tieing The Knot" bridal dolls. So far I've only made her dresses from scrap fabrics, with which her personal style is quite random and inconsistent. That doesn't make a good Vogue feature story I know!

It takes time for a young girl like her to figure out what style is really for her. Let her play with clothes for now! As her stylist (and mommy), I'll continue catering her imagination and looking forward to a fair lady to come.

* And no, there's no such a fragrance called Emerald from David Redman. David Redman is a luxury glassware brand, and besides their liquor glasses of all kinds, they also make stunning custom-made perfume bottles. The bottle I used for the back cover ads costs £250.00 - the bottle only!
