iSeed

Year: 2007
Team:
Mikko Aro, Mikko Reinkainen, Shanfan Huang, Ville Mensio, Willem Haen
Supervisor: Peter McGrory

Modern brand, the hidden treasure every company seeks for, happened to be the common ground between modern design (creativity) and modern business (strategy). Companies who play the two in harmony master the magic. Multi-disciplinary teams were formed during the study module Design Strategy and Innovation. Taking iPhone’s grand launch as a starting point, we explored the way to bridge creativity and strategy in a simulated business envirionment.
However, this case study is meant to explain what seemed to work but doesn't; rather than telling a story of triumph.

Design Process

I still love the final presentation we made - it was vivid and won the favor of the audience - but only when looking back at the process, did I realize our "positioning" step wasn't convincing.
Mass customization is essentially against Apple's core value. We let our "creative guts" overtake the integrity of Apple brand.
If this proposal were approved, it would have subverted the whole brand. Fortunately, this is only a study project. And the lesson was learned.
